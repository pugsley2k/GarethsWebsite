About Me

Let me introduce myself . . .

I'm Gareth Pugsley and I'm a freelance web designer/developer.

After many years studying graphic design and motion design at University I discovered that I personally was more interested in the layout of websites and the designs behind them. The experience though has given me a good insight into what customers are looking for in a design. I have transferred my skills as a motion designer to the wolrd wide web and hopefully that shows in my design. As far as experience goes I have spent a lot of time self-teaching. HTML, CSS and Javascript came naturally and ever since then I have been in love with improving my knowledge of the subject and learning more!
